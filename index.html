Research

Building practical automation for analog and custom IC design

Analog layout generation

Layout synthesis, migration, editing, and interactive human-machine workflows for analog and mixed-signal design.

Routing and placement

Hierarchical routing, placement optimization, and manual-design-compatible legalization for complex layout scenarios.

AI for EDA

Large language models, multi-agent systems, and learning-based design intent modeling for circuit automation.

Performance prediction

Geometry, netlist topology, and electro-physical co-modeling for standard-cell and circuit PPA prediction.
