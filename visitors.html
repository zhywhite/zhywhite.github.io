What changed

The old badge counter only exposes a total number, not historical coordinates. The live visitor map starts collecting map dots from the moment the shared widget is active.

Past badge-only visits cannot be retroactively placed on the map without logs from a tracking service.

Privacy

The public display should stay at city or country level. Raw IP addresses are not shown on the page.

For owner-only detailed analytics later, connect a private analytics dashboard.
